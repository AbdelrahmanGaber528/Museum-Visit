Dining at the Metropolitan Museum

Whether you're looking for a quick coffee, a leisurely lunch, or an elegant dining experience, the Metropolitan Museum offers a variety of food and drink options to enhance your visit. All our venues use locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients to create delicious meals and refreshments.

Rooftop Garden Restaurant

11:30 AM - 4:30 PM
5th Floor

Dine with spectacular views of Central Park. Our seasonal menu features chef-crafted dishes inspired by global cuisine, complemented by an extensive wine and cocktail selection.

The Great Hall Café

9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Ground Floor

Perfect for a quick bite, our Great Hall Café offers sandwiches, salads, snacks, and a variety of hot and cold beverages. Enjoy your refreshments in our elegant indoor seating area.
